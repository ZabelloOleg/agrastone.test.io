Eng

Christian, founder of Agrastone

also known as Chris, is a financial expert and coach with over ten years of international experience.It is committed to supporting its clients in achieving their medium and long-term financial objectives. Chris has served on financial committees, finance departments and boards of directors, providing valuable perspectives. He founded a company specializing in managing financial complexity with discretion.

Based in Lausanne, Chris has collaborated with various organizations such as the Solevo Group, IMD, the World Health Organization, Deloitte & Associés, the State of Vaud, Société Générale Corporate Investment Banking and CA Indosuez Wealth Management, among others. Chris studied at EMLYON Business School in France and Wake Forest University School of Business in the United States.

He is also a certified wealth manager and is affiliated with the Swiss Association of Financial Analysts.
